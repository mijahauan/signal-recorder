ðŸ“– Four-Panel Analysis Guide

Panel 1: SNR Comparison

Signal strength of WWV (1000 Hz, green) vs WWVH (1200 Hz, red).
Gaps = tone not detected (normal for weak propagation).

Panel 2: Power Ratio

WWV power minus WWVH power in dB.
Positive = WWV dominant | Negative = WWVH dominant | Near zero = Balanced

Panel 3: Differential Delay

Time difference between WWV and WWVH signal arrivals.
Typically 100-300ms. Variations indicate ionospheric path changes.

Panel 4: 440 Hz Station ID

WWV minute 2 (purple) vs WWVH minute 1 (cyan) 440 Hz tone detection.
Confirms station identity when both 1000 Hz and 1200 Hz are received.
